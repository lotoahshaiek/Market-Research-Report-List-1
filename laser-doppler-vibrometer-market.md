<p><p>The Laser Doppler Vibrometer (LDV) market is segmented into three main types: Single-point vibrometers, Scanning vibrometers, and Others. Scanning vibrometers, on the other hand, allow for mapping vibrations over an area, providing comprehensive data for dynamic assessments.
