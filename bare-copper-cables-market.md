<p><p>The bare copper cables market is categorized into three types based on their drawing processes: soft drawn, medium hard drawn, and hard drawn. Soft drawn cables are highly flexible, making them suitable for applications requiring bending and movement. Medium hard drawn cables strike a balance between flexibility and strength, offering moderate resistance to deformation. Hard drawn cables are the most rigid, providing high tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications where strength is paramount.</p></p>
