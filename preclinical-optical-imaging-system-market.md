<p><p>The preclinical optical imaging system market is categorized into fluorescence, bioluminescence, and other imaging types. Fluorescence imaging leverages fluorescent markers to visualize biological processes at the cellular and molecular levels, providing real-time insights. Bioluminescence imaging utilizes light emission from biological reactions to detect and quantify living organisms, often employed in drug efficacy and disease progression studies. Other imaging techniques may include multispectral imaging and reflectance methods, broadening the scope of in vivo imaging for various research applications.</p></p>
