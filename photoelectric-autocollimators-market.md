<p><p>Photoelectric autocollimators are precision optical instruments utilized for measuring angular displacements and alignments with high accuracy. These devices leverage the principle of autocollimation, whereby a beam of light is reflected from a surface and directed back to the source, allowing for precise alignment in various engineering and metrology applications. They are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, where exacting standards of quality and precision are essential.</p><p>The Photoelectric Autocollimators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demands for high-precision measurement tools in various sectors.
