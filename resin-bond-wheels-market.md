<p><p>The resin bond wheels market is categorized into four main types based on the abrasive materials used: Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN), Diamond, Silicon Carbide, and Aluminium Oxide. CBN wheels are highly effective for grinding hard materials, while Diamond wheels excel in cutting superhard materials. Silicon Carbide wheels are ideal for non-ferrous metals and offer a sharp cutting edge. Aluminium Oxide wheels are versatile and commonly used for general-purpose grinding applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on material properties.</p></p>
