<p><p>The photomask blank market is segmented into three types based on reflectivity. Low Reflectance Photomask Blanks are designed to minimize light reflection, enhancing imaging accuracy for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Medium Reflectance Photomask Blanks balance reflectivity and transmission, suitable for a range of technologies. High Reflectivity Photomask Blanks maximize light reflection, ideal for applications requiring high contrast and detail, such as deep ultraviolet lithography. Each type caters to specific lithographic needs, driving innovation in photomask technology.</p></p>
