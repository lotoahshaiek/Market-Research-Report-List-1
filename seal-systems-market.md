<p><p>Seal systems are categorized into various market types based on their design and application. Foam seal systems utilize compressible materials to create airtight barriers, ideal for insulation and vibration damping. Diaphragm seal systems are designed to protect sensitive instruments from harsh environments while maintaining pressure integrity. Compression seal systems rely on mechanical compression to create tight seals, suitable for high-stress applications. Other seal systems may include specialized solutions for unique industry needs, encompassing various materials and technologies to optimize performance and durability.</p></p>
