<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Fast Charger market comprises two main types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board Electric Vehicle Chargers (EVC) are integrated within the vehicle, converting AC power from standard outlets into DC power for battery charging. They provide convenience but generally have lower charging speeds. Off-board Electric Vehicle Chargers, on the other hand, are external units, often located in public charging stations. They offer higher power levels for faster charging times, supporting rapid refueling in urban and commercial settings.</p></p>
