<p><p>The Microfocus X-ray tubes market is primarily divided into two types: open tubes and sealed tubes. Open tube X-ray systems allow for direct access to the electron beam and target material, offering flexibility in adjustments and enhanced performance for specific applications. In contrast, sealed tube systems are hermetically sealed, providing a controlled environment that protects the components from contamination and wear. Both types are utilized in various industries, including medical imaging, non-destructive testing, and material analysis, each with distinct advantages.</p></p>
