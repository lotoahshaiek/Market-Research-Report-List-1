<p><strong>The Transient Elastography Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration Controlled Transient Elastography (VCTE)</li><li>Controlled Attenuation Parameter(CAP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transient Elastography Device market includes two primary types: Vibration Controlled Transient Elastography (VCTE) and Controlled Attenuation Parameter (CAP). VCTE measures liver stiffness using low-frequency vibrations to assess fibrosis, providing essential diagnostic information for liver diseases. CAP complements this by evaluating liver fat content, helping in the assessment of steatosis. Together, these technologies enhance non-invasive liver evaluation, making them vital tools in the diagnosis and management of chronic liver conditions while improving patient outcomes through accurate assessments.</p></p>
