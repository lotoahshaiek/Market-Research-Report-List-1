<p><p>The server rack market can be categorized into wall-mounted and detached types. Wall-mounted racks are compact and designed to save floor space, making them ideal for smaller environments such as offices or limited server rooms. They offer easy access to equipment while maintaining a clean appearance. Detached racks, on the other hand, are standalone units that provide more extensive storage and organization for larger setups. They facilitate better airflow and cooling, making them suitable for data centers and enterprise-level installations.</p></p>
