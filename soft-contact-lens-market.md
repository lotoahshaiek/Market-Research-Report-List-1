Additionally, the shift toward online retail channels and the growing popularity of cosmetic lenses are adding momentum to market growth. Recent trends also highlight an increased focus on materials that promote eye health, such as those offering enhanced oxygen permeability and moisture retention. As consumer awareness of eye care rises, the soft contact lens market is poised for sustained growth and innovation in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Daily Replacement Lens</li><li>Weekly Replacement Lens</li><li>Monthly Replacement Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft contact lens market is categorized into three main types based on replacement frequency. Daily replacement lenses are designed for single-use, promoting hygiene and convenience, as they are discarded each night. Weekly replacement lenses should be replaced every week, offering a balance between cost-effectiveness and comfort. Monthly replacement lenses are meant to be used for up to a month, requiring regular cleaning and care but offering a more economical option for long-term users. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
