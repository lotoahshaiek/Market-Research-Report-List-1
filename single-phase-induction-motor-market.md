<p><strong>The Single-Phase Induction Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split Phase Motors</li><li>Shaded-Pole Induction Motors</li><li>Reluctance-Start Induction Motor</li><li>Repulsion-Start Induction Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase induction motor market includes several types catering to various applications. Split-phase motors utilize two windings for starting, ideal for low-torque appliances. Shaded-pole induction motors feature a simple design and are primarily used in small fans and low-power applications. Reluctance-start motors combine features of shaded-pole and split-phase designs, offering improved efficiency. Repulsion-start motors use a commutator for better starting torque, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Each type serves unique operational needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
