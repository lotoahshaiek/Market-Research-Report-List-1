<p><p>The IV flush syringe market encompasses various types designed for specific medical applications. Saline IV flush syringes utilize sterile saline solution to maintain venous access, clear IV lines, and hydrate patients. Heparin IV flush syringes contain a low dose of heparin, an anticoagulant that prevents blood clotting in catheters and IV lines. Both types are essential for safe and effective intravenous therapy, helping to ensure proper medication delivery and minimizing complications associated with prolonged venous access.</p></p>
