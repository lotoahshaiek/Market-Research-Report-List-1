<p><strong>The UAV LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanners</li><li>Navigation & Positioning System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UAV LiDAR market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into laser scanners and navigation & positioning systems. Laser scanners capture high-resolution, three-dimensional spatial data by emitting laser beams, making them essential for applications like topographic mapping and vegetation analysis. Navigation and positioning systems, including GNSS and inertial measurement units, ensure precise geolocation of UAVs during data collection, enhancing accuracy and reliability of the LiDAR data. Together, these components enable effective survey and mapping solutions across diverse industries.</p></p>
