<p><p>The image sensor chips market primarily comprises two types: Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them suitable for high-end applications like professional cameras. Conversely, CMOS sensors are more energy-efficient and cost-effective, enabling their widespread use in smartphones and consumer electronics. The market trends highlight a growing preference for CMOS due to advancements in technology and increasing demand for mobile imaging solutions.</p></p>
