<p><strong>The Industrial Current Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-loop Sensors</li><li>Open-loop Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial current sensor market is categorized into closed-loop and open-loop sensors. Closed-loop sensors utilize feedback mechanisms to provide precise current measurements, often featuring higher accuracy and stability, making them suitable for critical applications. In contrast, open-loop sensors measure current without feedback, typically offering simpler designs and lower costs, though with reduced precision. Both types serve diverse industries, catering to varying requirements for efficiency, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness in monitoring electrical currents.</p></p>
