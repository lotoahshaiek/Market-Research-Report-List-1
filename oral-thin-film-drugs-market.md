<p><p>The Oral Thin Film Drugs market comprises sublingual films and fully dissolving dental/buccal films. Sublingual films are designed to be placed under the tongue, allowing for rapid absorption into the bloodstream, ideal for fast-acting medications. Fully dissolving dental/buccal films adhere to the gums or inner cheek, providing sustained release and localized effects, often used for pain management or treatment of oral conditions. Both types enhance patient compliance due to their convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
