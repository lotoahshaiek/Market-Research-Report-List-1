<p><p>Molecular imaging agents are categorized into several types based on their size and composition. Engineered protein fragments offer enhanced specificity and stability. Aptamers are oligonucleotides designed to bind targets with high affinity. Nanoparticles utilize nanoscale materials for imaging and therapeutic purposes. Additional categories may include antibodies and other biomolecules, broadening the spectrum of imaging applications.</p></p>
