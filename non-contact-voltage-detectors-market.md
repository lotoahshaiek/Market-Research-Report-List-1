<p><strong>The Non-contact Voltage Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Magnetic Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact voltage detectors operate mainly through two types: capacitive and magnetic induction. Capacitive detectors sense voltage fields by detecting electric charge changes, allowing users to identify live wires without contact. In contrast, magnetic induction detectors detect electromagnetic fields generated by current flow, providing an alternative method for safely identifying voltage presence. Both types enhance electrical safety, serving electricians, technicians, and DIY enthusiasts by offering a quick and safe means of voltage confirmation without direct contact with electrical components.</p></p>
