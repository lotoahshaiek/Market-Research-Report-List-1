<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Induction Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low voltage AC motor market primarily comprises synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors synchronize their speed with the supply frequency, offering high efficiency and precision in applications requiring constant speed. They typically use permanent magnets or electromagnets for operation. In contrast, induction motors rely on electromagnetic induction to generate torque, making them more widely used due to their simplicity and robustness. Both types play crucial roles in industrial automation, HVAC systems, and various other applications, driving efficiency and performance.</p></p>
