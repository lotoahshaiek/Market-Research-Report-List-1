<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Actuators</li><li>Electric Actuators</li><li>Pneumatic Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military aircraft actuation systems market is primarily segmented into hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic actuators. Hydraulic actuators are widely used for their high force output and reliability in extreme conditions, making them ideal for flight control surfaces. Electric actuators offer advantages in terms of weight reduction, precision, and reduced maintenance, aligning with modern aircraft design trends. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air for actuation, providing fast response times, though they are less common in advanced military applications. Each type serves specific operational requirements.</p></p>
