<p><p>Gift certificate cards are categorized based on their usability and restrictions. Universal Accepted Open Loop cards can be used at various merchants, offering flexibility. E-Gifting cards are digital and allow users to send gifts electronically. Restaurant Closed Loop cards are specific to eateries, usable only within their networks. Retail Closed Loop cards can be redeemed at particular retail outlets. Lastly, Miscellaneous Closed Loop cards cater to niche markets or services, often limited to specific vendors or locations.</p></p>
