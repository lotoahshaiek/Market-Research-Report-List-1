<p><strong>The Commercial Electric Juicing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Masticating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial electric juicing machines market features two primary types: centrifugal and masticating juicers. Centrifugal juicers operate quickly, using high-speed blades to extract juice by spinning fruits and vegetables, making them ideal for fast-paced environments. Masticating juicers, on the other hand, utilize a slow grinding mechanism to crush produce, preserving more nutrients and yielding higher juice quality. While centrifugal juicers offer speed and efficiency, masticating models excel in nutrient retention and juice shelf life, catering to different consumer needs.</p></p>
