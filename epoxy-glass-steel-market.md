<p><strong>The Epoxy Glass Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Contact Production Process</li><li>Dry Press Forming Production Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Epoxy Glass Steel market encompasses two primary production processes: Wet Contact Production and Dry Press Forming. The Wet Contact Production process involves combining epoxy resin with glass fibers in a liquid state, allowing for intricate designs and strong bonding. In contrast, Dry Press Forming utilizes pre-preg materials, where fibers are pre-impregnated with resin, then pressed into molds, resulting in a denser and more uniform product. Both methods cater to diverse applications, driven by their material properties and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
