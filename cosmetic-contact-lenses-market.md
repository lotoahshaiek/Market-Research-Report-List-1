<p><p>The cosmetic contact lenses market comprises three main types: rigid, soft, and hybrid lenses. Rigid contact lenses are durable and provide crisp vision, often used for astigmatism correction. Soft contact lenses are flexible and comfortable, catering to various prescriptions and cosmetic enhancements, popular for changing eye color or adding effects. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid gas-permeable center with a soft outer ring, offering the clarity of rigid lenses alongside the comfort of soft ones, appealing to a broad consumer base.</p></p>
