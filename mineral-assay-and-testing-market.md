<p><strong>The Mineral Assay and Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Testing</li><li>Chemical Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral assay and testing market is primarily divided into two types: physical testing and chemical testing. Physical testing assesses the physical properties of minerals, such as size distribution, density, and specific gravity, which are crucial for processing and application. Chemical testing, on the other hand, analyzes the elemental composition and purity of minerals through techniques like spectrometry or chromatography. Both testing methods are essential in ensuring quality control, compliance with regulations, and optimizing mineral extraction processes.</p></p>
