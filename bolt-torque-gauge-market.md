<p><ul><li>Handheld Detector</li><li>Fixed Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bolt torque gauge market can be divided into two primary types: handheld detectors and fixed detectors. Handheld detectors are portable tools that allow users to measure torque applied to bolts on-site, offering flexibility and ease of use in various applications. In contrast, fixed detectors are permanently installed systems used in manufacturing or assembly lines, providing continuous monitoring and precise torque measurements. Both types enhance safety and efficacy in maintaining the integrity of bolted connections in different operational environments.</p></p>
